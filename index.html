urban planning

Urban planning has an important role in several cities around the world. It helps in every aspect of an urban development and its civilians dialy life's quality. Many cities stand out in this matter such as Barcelona, Amsterdam, Zurich or La Plata.

This area developpes a city according to the community's requirements or needs in terms of expanding and/or revitalising specific areas, infrastructure and systems. In european cities we can appreciate a remarkable architecture conceived in a well structured plan of the city, which helps to achieve a better functionality of the city's space.

Aside from that, in latin american cities we can evidence a different progress compared to european cities in terms of urban planning. For my final project I will portray different european and latin american cities to showcase the contrast between these.
